Heating Oil Price Plans

* Limited time offer *

Garrett Pillsbury has been providing plumbing and heating services to the York county community since 1959. That’s over 55 years of dependable service.

A year ago, we extended our services to include heating oil and propane delivery, giving our customers the fullest offering of plumbing, heating, and air conditioning services around. Since then our heating oil and propane trucks have been busy making deliveries all over town.

We're a locally owned and operated company, which means 100% of the business you do with us is reinvested back into the community. And like any trusted neighbor, we'll always be there to make sure you have what you need.

Pick your plan

Plan #1: Pre-buy Fixed Price

  • Heating oil at $3.399 per gallon.
  • All gallons contracted for would be delivered and billed at a fixed rate of $3.399 per gallon.
  • Any gallons delivered in excess of gallons contracted would be billed at that day’s credit price.
  • Automatic delivery included.

This is the right plan for you if you're able to pay the full amount up front, which protects you against fluxuating prices. To sign up, fill out the Pre-buy Fixed Price form and mail it to us with a check for the total amount.

Plan #2: Budget With a Cap

  • Heating oil at $3.499 per gallon with a CAP Fee of 25¢ per gallon (Non-refundable).
  • Pay monthly over 10 or 12 months (your choice) with CAP Protection.
  • All deliveries for gallons contracted would be billed at our current cash price on the day of delivery not to exceed $3.499 per gallon.
  • Any gallons delivered in excess of gallons contracted would be billed at that day’s credit price.
  • Automatic delivery included.

This is the right plan for you if you want to make monthly payments up to a certain amount. To sign up for this plan, fill out the Budget With a Cap form and mail it to us with a check for the first month's cap fee. The first full payment is due by July 1st, 2014.

Plan #3: Budget Without a Cap

  • Monthly budget payments initially calculated using a rate of $3.499 per gallon.
  • Pay monthly over 10 or 12 months (your choice).
  • All deliveries for gallons contracted would be billed at our current cash price on the day of delivery.
  • Any gallons delivered in excess of gallons contracted would be billed at that day’s credit price.
  • Automatic delivery included.

This is the right plan for you if you want to make monthly payments. To sign up for this plan, fill out the Budget Without a Cap form and mail it to us. The first full payment is due by July 1st, 2014.

Additional information

Each plan comes with a few extra perks.

All Garrett Pillsbury heating oil and propane customers receive a discounted annual boiler or furnace tuneup of $150.00 (normally $177.50) and an additional 10% off all kitchen and bath fixtures purchased through our retail store.

Financing is available.

You can apply for financing by completing our financing application and sending it along with your plan's sign up form.
