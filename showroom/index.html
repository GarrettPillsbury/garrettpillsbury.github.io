Showroom

Come in Monday through Friday from 8AM to 4PM or Saturday from 8AM to 12PM and see how you can upgrade your home.

Make your everyday life better.

It's amazing what a new showerhead can do for your everyday well-being. Our showroom is full of ways to make your everyday home life better, including faucets, vanities, steam machines and much more.

Experts on hand to help you find what you need.

Let our showroom experts walk you through all the major brands to find exactly what you need. They'll ask you about the intended use, who will be using it (including if children will use it often), what your price point is, and when you'd like to install it.

Discounted installation and 1-year warranty.

Buying from Garrett Pillsbury gives you the option to have us install it for you at a discounted labor rate with a 1-year labor warranty. Even if you prefer to install it yourself, all of our products come with a 1-year warranty in addition to the manufacturer's.
