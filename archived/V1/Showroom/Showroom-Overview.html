Showroom

We want to welcome you to the recently renovated Garrett Pillsbury Plumbing & Heating showroom!

This is the place where you can meet with knowledgeable staff in person who can assist you with all of your plumbing & heating needs. We have designed our showroom to help you visualize your next project.

Whether it is as simple as picking out a kitchen faucet to creating a new theme, our staff can help you each and every step of the way. We have an in-house designer ready to assist you as well as experienced technicians who can answer any question you may have.

Our redesigned showroom includes a wide range of products from recognized industry manufacturers for your kitchen, bath, and home heating system. Come in and experience the bold look of Kohler first hand.
