Generator Installation

Why should you consider installing a whole house generator?

For the homeowner, backup power can mean the difference between staying put and moving out or not being a hostage to your home so that you can take that getaway trip even in the face of a pending storm. With a residential power system – a home standby generator or a residential emergency generator – you’ve got a potent ally on your side when the lights go out – power for life without interruption. By installing a residential standby generator you will be insuring that you and your family can continue to live in comfort when the home experiences the harsh weather conditions this area of the country is known for. As we both know, some of these power outages can last for days. You can live with a little more peace of mind; I wouldn’t purchase or build a home without one! It gets even better should you be able to build the cost of an installation into the financing package for your home. It could cost you less than $50.00 per month.

Why should you have GP Power Systems, a division of Garrett Pillsbury, install the generator?

You will find that we provide the most comprehensive installation offered. An installation involves the acquisition and provision of the generator, propane tanks and propane, trenching, gas piping and testing, electrical wiring, start-up (by factory trained technicians), registration of the generator for warranty purposes, annual maintenance and warranty servicing (if needed).

If we perform the installation, you will deal directly with:

  • GP Power Systems
  • A fuel company to furnish the tank and propane

If you have someone else perform the installation, you will deal with a combination of:

  • The company providing the generator
  • An electrician
  • A fuel company to furnish the tank and propane
  • A service company for long-term issues

We have developed a highly efficient process for installing these generators and as a result are able to minimize your cost as compared to when the various pieces of an installation are handled by different parties.
