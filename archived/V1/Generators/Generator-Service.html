Generator Service

Most importantly we don’t leave you in the lurch after the installation. Unlike many other installers, we service what we sell, year after year. The success of our business model is the development and maintenance of a long term relationship with our customers. We have spent thousands of dollars for factory training and certification of our service technicians and in having a significant amount of repair parts in our inventory.

Over the last several years we have received many phone calls from people including master electricians who have installed a generator for a homeowner only to have a problem with the start-up or later during the warranty period. In most of those cases the electricians are unable to resolve or handle the problems whether it is because of a lack of; manufacturer’s training and certification, experience or their ability to obtain repair parts. In each of those cases we have been asked to step in and resolve the problem. If it is part of the service the installer promised in his contract with the homeowner we have no other choice but to charge the electrician or homeowner. If it turns out to be a potential warranty issue we will bill the homeowner and credit them upon receiving a credit from the manufacturer. In most of these cases the lines of service and compensation have become blurred and messy.

Your interests are best protected by an installer who offers a comprehensive installation package and is in it for the long term!
