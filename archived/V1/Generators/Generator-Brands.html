Supported Generator Brands

Kohler

Kohler has offered backup generators since the 1920’s when they developed the world’s first engine-drive automatic generator. Kohler commercial grade residential generators offer clean power for sensitive home electronics, fast power to get you up and running in virtually no time and reliable backup power to ensure your life goes on even when utility power doesn’t. Features include an automatic switch when power is lost, a quiet operation, powerful performance, digital controls, and a faster response.

Kohler and many in the industry believe that the research, testing, production methods and components used in assembling their generators are superior and places their products among the best in quality on the market. Although this allows them to charge a premium for their product, in our eyes it is well worth it. In support of this they offer a superior 5-year limited warranty.

Generac

Generac is solely focused on power generation and has been for over 50 years. They are considered the largest manufacturer of generators in terms of number units and have an extensive nationwide dealer network. They also offer one of the broadest lines of home generators. Because of this mass production they are able to offer their generators at a lower price point. The generators come with a 3-year limited warranty.
