Generators

Kohler has offered backup generators since the 1920s when they developed the world's first engine-driven automatic generator. Kohler residential generators offer clean power for sensitive home electronics, fast power to get you up and running in virtually no time, and reliable backup power to ensure your life goes on even when utility power doesn't.

Features include an automatic switch when power is lost, a quiet operation, powerful performance, digital controls, and a faster response. All this is backed by a superior 5-year limited warranty.

For the homeowner, backup power can mean the difference between staying put and moving out. With a Kohler commercial-grade residential power system – a home standby generator or a residential emergency generator – you've got a potent ally on your side when the lights go out and power for life without interruption.
